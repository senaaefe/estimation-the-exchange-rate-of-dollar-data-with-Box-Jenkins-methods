# estimation-the-exchange-rate-of-dollar-data-with-Box-Jenkins-methods
 In project, forward estimation has been based on database of Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) contains the exchange rate of dollar data from 1950 to 2020. For the sake of reliable estimation the exchange rate of dollar data has been used in parts to avert the change in Turkish Lira currency in consequence of the amendment in Turkish Lira effective in 01.01.2005.
